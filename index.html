Vision Geometry Transformer

VGT is a Multi-Head Geometric Attention module designed to understand geometric relations in images through graph-based representation.

Developed by Dr. Abdulmajid Naaman and the Athena Research Team, VGT-Core v0.2 integrates edge-aware attention to process vision data with higher geometric fidelity.
